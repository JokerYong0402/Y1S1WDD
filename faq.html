Frequently Asked Questions

Why order with our Baker?
To guarantee that each cake, bread and cookie is hand-made freshly, from scratch, and to order, we carefully choose our network of outstanding bakers. Because of this, when our cakes are delivered to your home, they are still moist and tasty. In addition, we can deliver 6 days a week with only a 24-hour notice for the majority of products. Most dietary restrictions are met, and our helpful customer support team is always available.
How do we ensure quality?
Firstly, we only employ bakers who are equipped to operate a baking business. A baker must possess the following in order to be accepted into our community:
  1. Food registration approval with their local council
  2. The relevant food and hygiene certificate
  3. A body of previous work demonstrating their skills as a baker
Secondly, every baker for every cake we feature is required to meet our strict standards for all of our items. We keep track of every order and ensure that every cake meets our requirements. But as each cake is prepared by hand, there could occasionally be a very slight deviation from the product image. Lastly, we consider client comments carefully and monitor baker performance. When we incredibly rarely fall short of customer standards, we instantly try our hardest to make it right.
How can I place an order?
You can place your order in two ways:
  • Via our online shop. Browse, select your cake, personalize with add-ons / message, and make your payment online.
  • You can visit our actual store to see the food physically.
Where do you deliver?
We only deliver in west Malaysia.
What happens if I need to amend my order?
Please contact us by email at thebreadbasket@gmail.com or call our contact number(+603 473 9560) within 24 hours after placing the order. Please make sure to add the order number in the subject line of your email so that we can quickly review the information and notify our bakers. Note that while we will try our best to accommodate your adjustments, we cannot guarantee that alterations to your original order will be accepted without the baker's permission. The amendment is confirmed upon receipt of the appropriate extra payment, which may have a cost impact depending on the amendment.
What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase? What is your refund/complaint policy?
Our goal is to give each and every one of our customers wonderful cakes, breads and cookies with excellent service. Senior management evaluates every complaint individually. We strive to settle disputes in a fair and impartial manner, taking into account the worries of both the client and the baker. Within 24 hours of receiving the cake, if for any reason you are dissatisfied with your order, kindly write us a feedback with your order number. Any complaint that is made more than 72 hours after the cake is delivered will not be taken into consideration. If there is a glaring issue with the cake, we apologize and will try to fix the matter. If we are unable to fix the problem, we shall make a good faith effort to negotiate and agree on the proper discount under the circumstances. Additionally, if you like it, you can also send us your feedback.
What do I do if my order is late or doesn't arrive?
Each cake is delivered within the allotted 24-hour window of time. To contact our customer care team, send an email to thebreadbasket@gmail.com with your order number in the odd event that your order does not arrive as expected. Alternatively, call us on +603 473 9560. Our opening hours are 7am - 5pm (Mon-Sat) .
What if my cake arrives damaged?
Please call us immediately on +603 473 9560 and send the accompanying photos to thebreadbasket@gmail.com if your cake came in damaged condition. If this occurs outside of business hours for customer care, email us and we will respond as soon as we can.
How does delivery work? Do you offer Collection?
Our bakers will charge RM5 as delivery fee. We have partnered with several delivery companies, who specialize in delivering fragile cakes and other bakes. Except in extraordinary circumstances, we do not offer collections.
What is the best way to store my cake / cookies / bread?
Cake:
- We recommend you enjoy the cakes within 48 hours of purchase. Keep your cake in the fridge if you plan to serve it the following day, but for optimal results, let it stay at room temperature for at least two hours before serving.

Cookies:
- Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or in the freezer for up to 2 months and reheat it in the oven while serving.

Bread:
- We advise you to consume it within three days and keep it at room temperature. You also can heat it up for a better taste.
If I'm having trouble completing payment. What should I do?
Please send an email to thebreadbasket@gmail.com with your order number in the subject line to our customer care department. You can also give us a call at +603 473 9560. Our opening hours are 7a.m. to 5p.m. (Mon-Sat).
