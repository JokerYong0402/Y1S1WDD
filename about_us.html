The Story of The Bread Basket

- Our mission -

To make the world a better place.

- Who we are? -

Hi! We are a group of students who just graduated, and our dream is to open a bakery to sell all the varieties that customers need for their love.
We are an online bakery shop that provides cakes, breads and cookies.

Our corporation is made up of a group of committed bakers and young pastry chefs that attended an advanced pastry course.
We always concentrated on creating more desserts in the French pastry style using regional ingredients.

We started this small business after we graduated from college. We have always loved baking.
We think that bakers are very lucky people. We have a profession that provides both pleasure and nourishment to a community. It is like therapy for us.
What was intended as a side income business grew tremendously thanks to our crowd favorites, particularly our chocolate cake, so we operate a physical shop.
While others serve you with normal and ordinary stuff, we work to give you only the best tasting for any of your special occasions.

- About -

And believe us when we say we're experienced, because we have been sidekicks in the kitchen for longer than you'd guess!
We've even experimented in thousands of possible ways, topped with bucket loads of moral support from friends and family alike to make it so far!
Today, we had our own small space for a bakery shop where we could display our special creations.

Our breads and cakes are freshly baked every morning and made with organic whole grains and natural leavening, without added sugar or preservatives.
All breads and pastries are made by hand, from scratch, using centuries-old techniques and pure ingredients.

We also add a menu with buns and cookies for physical customers.
If you want to try it, you can also order it through our website.
The buns and cookies from the online order will have a nice package.
You can send them to your lover.

We still take whole cake orders online (our core business since Day One) and we also accept custom cakes based on customers' needs.
You can also search for our shop on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter or contact us by phone.
